Customer Ratings

In this most competitive environment today, identifying the premium customers and their importance in your business to sustain the optimum profitable growth has become the most inevitable task for any business.

Customer Ratings application aids businesses to have detailed customer insights, plan targeted strategies leading to winning highest leads, sales and all in one maintain optimum Customer Retention Strategy too. While deciding the ratings and score of customers various parameters like average monthly sales, average monthly sales return, due of the amount of invoices, the number of due invoices, amount of invoices paid after due dates are considered which says that the rating score given to the customer is highly accurate to identify their behavior of purchase.

Thus, Customer Rating is the solution that considers all the parameters of a customer in one record and does an analysis of customers which ultimately helps business owners to take necessary decisions leading to optimum profit.
